🎓 Teacher Training Programmes

Our accredited teacher training centre — located within Future Grads International Academy — offers specialized diploma and certificate programmes in early childhood education, child psychology, and Montessori methodology.

📘 Course 1: Certificate in Montessori Education

  • Introduction to early childhood
  • Montessori principles & teaching tools
  • Classroom setup & observation
  • Child growth & behavior

📗 Course 2: Diploma in Montessori Method

  • Includes all Certificate content
  • Environment & classroom maintenance
  • Art & craft activities
  • Child psychology (basic)

📙 Course 3: Diploma in Child Psychology

  • Intro to Child Psychology
  • Behavioral patterns and traits
  • Moral, emotional & cognitive growth
  • Biological bases of development

📕 Course 4: Diploma in Childcare Management

  • Emotional needs of children
  • Programming & activity planning
  • Child safety & hygiene
  • First Aid training

👩‍🏫 About the Course Directress
