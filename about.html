📖 About Future Grads

Future Grads International Academy was founded in 2001 by Mrs. Yasmin Faiz, a passionate educator with a vision to create a nurturing space for children’s development.

With over two decades of experience in early childhood education, we are now a trusted institution in Moratuwa — loved by parents and remembered by students long after they graduate.

🧪 STEAM Education

We follow a hands-on, play-based learning model rooted in the STEAM framework: Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, and Math. Our goal is to develop critical thinking, creativity, and curiosity — even in the earliest years.

👩‍🏫 Our Team

Our teachers are trained in Montessori methodology, child psychology, and modern educational practices. With patience, love, and professional training, our team supports every child’s unique growth path.
